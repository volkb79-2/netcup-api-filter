What are recovery codes?

Recovery codes are backup codes that can be used to access your account if you lose access to your primary two-factor authentication method (like your phone or authenticator app).

  • Each code can only be used once
  • 10 codes are generated at a time
  • Store them in a safe, secure location
  • Generating new codes invalidates all old codes
